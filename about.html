About me

I am a User Experience and Product Designer. I love brainstorming and crafting creative design solutions. My background in architecture, in combination with my UX studies, has inspired me to give extra attention to details, become more empathetic, and create human-centric design. I enjoy working in cross-disciplinary teams to improve myself along with my teammates and to solve tough problems in the best way possible.

Growing up I enjoyed playing all kinds of sports, especially basketball. As a college athlete, I played for a team that helped me develop a sportsmanship spirit and my ability to become a team player.

Other than that, furry friends hold a special place in my heart and my new found love is gardening. I am always in search of new adventures and challenges which drive my enthusiasm and curiosity.
