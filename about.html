About me

I am a UX/UI designer. Brainstorming and creating creative design solutions is something that excites me the most. My background in architecture has molded me to give extra attention to details, helped to become more empathetic and create human centric design.

Growing up I enjoyed playing all kinds of sports, especially basketball. Being in a college team and playing for a team has helped me to develop a sportsmanship spirit and become a team player. Working in a team to improve myself along with my teammates and solve tough problems in the best way possible aspires me.

Else than that, furry friends hold a special place in my heart and my new found love is gardening. I am always in search of new adventures and challenges which drive my enthusiasm and curiosity.
